Understanding ETH, stETH & wstETH

ETH — Native Ethereum. No yield unless you stake it.

stETH — Lido's liquid staking token. It rebases daily: your balance increases as staking rewards accrue (~3-4% APR).

wstETH — "Wrapped" stETH. Balance stays constant, but each wstETH becomes worth more stETH over time. Better for DeFi and L2s.

Multi-chain support
wstETH is available on Ethereum, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base & Polygon. This tracker checks all chains simultaneously to show your full position.
How to Find Your Entry Rate

Click Auto-Detect to scan your on-chain transfers and estimate your entry rate automatically. Or find it manually:

  1. Go to Arbiscan (or relevant explorer) and find your original swap transaction
  2. Look at the token amounts: WETH sent and wstETH received
  3. Calculate: Entry Rate = WETH ÷ wstETH

Example: 33 WETH → 26.94 wstETH = 1.2247 entry rate
